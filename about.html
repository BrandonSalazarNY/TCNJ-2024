Our Business

CycleVend enables users to choose from four common waste streams, allowing them to store items in a virtual recycling bin that displays relevant information about each product..

App Usage

Inspired by the lack of streamlined recycling in my college residence building, CycleVend aims to transform a waste stream into profit. This initiative not only addresses environmental concerns but also capitalizes on the opportunity to generate income from recyclable materials.
